An alternative--and much better--way to implement a scavenger hunt. Prompts you zoom in and click on President Obama, congratulating you when you do. Must be zoomed in sufficiently for the click to be honored. Detects clicks on an invisible Seadragon overlay placed on top of Obama. [source]
